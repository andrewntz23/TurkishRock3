Write, reporter, write!: Politics and Language of Turkish Rock Music

This is a project done for the Computational Humanities class at the University of Pittsburgh. We are examining the politics of several Turkish artists involved in the Anatolian rock movement including

  • Selda Bagcan
  • Cem Karaca
  • Barış Manço
  • Erkin koray
  • Edip Akbayram
  • Ersen Dinleten
